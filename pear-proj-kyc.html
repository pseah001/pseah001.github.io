2. Aligning on the short & long term direction

Situation

Initially, business team requested to redesign the case management and case routing flow. However, due to KYC upcoming reorganisation, we were uncertain how that will impact their roles & responsibilities.​

Task

To mitigate risk, I suggested shifting towards features with low dependency on the reorganisation. Prioritising high impact and low effort items. Together with KYC team and business team, we prioritised designing:

  • A master profile pulling data across 7 systems
  • Allowing minor updates to go through a partial review case

Results

  • We had alignment on short vs long term plan.

Design

1. Modular edits for minor updates

Problem: Full review even for minor edits

  • High volume of review cases: ~ 12,711
  • Manually combine data from 7 systems & documents for each full review case
  • Each full review case has manual input > 200 fields
  • Each full review case takes > 8.5h

Solution

  • Allow modular edits for cases requiring minor edits
  • Auto pull data across 7 systems into 1 master customer profile for KYC team to review during prep work stage
  • KYC team can justify reasonableness of changes with free text field, accessable at any section of case

Result

  • 12% reduction in cases needing full review
  • 1h of work instead of 8.5h for the 12% of cases
  • 3.84 full time employee saved annually
